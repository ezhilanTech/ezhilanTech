FSIh

Transcending Knowledge through cultural heritage studie

The Foundation for South Indian Heritage (FSIH) is formulated to foster a holistic approach for a sustainable heritage conservation and management. The primary purpose of the FSIH is to train the young professionals on the conservation of heritage-built structures and to engage in R&D and consultancy services in restoration of existing heritage structures…

Vision

To harness the power of cultural heritage in the interest of Built Environment empowering the citizens to contribute to socio economic sustainable community. The FSIH also strategically to place the best practices to manage deterioration of heritage structure through research, education and training and play leadership role in decision making organization…

Mission

  • Research, study and promote heritage structures and its cultural importance
  • Coordinate with experts to understand the critical issues in preserving and suggest technology
  • Train the next generation with tools for implementation and to strengthen the community professionals


Objectives:

  • A non-profitable organisation focused on promoting and preserving built heritage.
  • Imparting knowledge and capacity building by conducting Courses & Training programs to students, contractors, engineers, officials, etc
  • To undertake research and study of materials used in heritage structures.
  • Bringing balance between development and Heritage conservation through detailed management.
  • Documentation with photos, developing plans, section, elevation drawings etc, record the existing conditions. and proposal, advisory for renovation and rejuvenation.
  • Facilitates learing of new softwares to understand and decode the cultural heritage.
  • Skill development programs for masons, stapathis, carpenters, painters etc.
  • Adoptation of newer technologies ( BHIM, Radar, Nano, 3d ) , Tools and plants , Scientific methods etc
  • Bringing out publications both print and digital versions and guidebooks, Do-it-Yourself (DiY) books on preservation, conservation, and renovation of heritage structure
  • Publishing the good works in leading magazine/journals for knowledge dissemination
  • Engaging in Consultancy services for conservation, restoration and regeneration of heritage structures.
